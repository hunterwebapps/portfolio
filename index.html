03 / Where We Fit

Where we fit best.

We do our best work with operators who can measure the cost of manual work, missed exceptions, or reporting lag — and who are not looking for a WMS replacement vendor.

Strong fit
  • Independent 3PLs and fulfillment operators with legacy systems they cannot easily replace
  • Retailer-heavy or marketplace-heavy accounts
  • Manual portal work, copy/paste, and exception chasing
  • Chargeback, PIFOT, on-time, fill-rate, or ship-window pressure
  • Operators relying on spreadsheets, email, and tribal knowledge to bridge system gaps
  • Leaders who can measure the cost of manual work, missed exceptions, or reporting lag
Poor fit
  • You mainly need a new WMS implementation
  • You want generic software development unrelated to 3PL operations
  • You want an AI pilot without a measurable operational workflow
  • You only need a dashboard, not a workflow change
  • You cannot provide access to the systems, data, and process owners needed to validate ROI
04 / Engagement

How we de-risk the work.

We do not start by writing code. We start by proving that the workflow is frequent enough, painful enough, and measurable enough to justify automation or an operational layer.

The four steps

From workflow fit to managed expansion.

Each step decides whether the next one is worth doing. No big-bang commitments and no contracts that outrun the evidence.

Workflow Fit Call
Free

A focused conversation to determine whether your workflow has enough volume, risk, and operational drag to justify a diagnostic.

  • Confirm systems involved
  • Discuss current cost, risk, and ownership
  • Decide whether a diagnostic is warranted
→ Leads to Diagnostic

Margin Leakage Diagnostic

  • Pilot recommendation and implementation plan
→ Leads to Pilot
